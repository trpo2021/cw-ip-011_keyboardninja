The moon is the only natural satellite of the earth. according to scientists, the moon used to be part of the earth, which broke off after a large cosmic body hit it billions of years ago. on the moon, the gravity is there, but so weak that astronauts, even in heavy space gear, can jump high. If a person on earth weighs, for example, 60 kg, then his weight on the moon is reduced to about 10 kg!

the largest cave system in the world is considered to be mamontova, it was discovered about 200 years ago in the basin of a tributary of the Mississippi river. It is a complex multi-storey maze consisting of grottos, passages and wells located on five tiers. by 1900, about 200 passages of the mammoth cave were known. now it is a national park, which is included in the list of world heritage sites.
